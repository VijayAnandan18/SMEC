The Harbour Engineering Section

At Selwin Majestic Engineering Consultancy, we specialize in harbour engineering, focusing on the design and construction of port facilities that ensure safe and efficient maritime operations. Our expertise encompasses everything from the development of breakwaters and docks to the implementation of navigational aids and environmental management systems. By utilizing cutting-edge technology and sustainable practices, we aim to enhance the functionality and resilience of harbours, supporting both commercial and recreational activities while protecting marine ecosystems.

Harbour Engineering Services

  • Design and construction of port facilities
  • Breakwater and dock development
  • Implementation of navigational aids
  • Environmental management systems
  • Harbour planning and feasibility studies
  • Hydrodynamic modeling and analysis
  • Maintenance and rehabilitation of harbour structures

Harbour Engineering Technical Strengths

  • Advanced structural analysis and design
  • Wave and current interaction studies
  • Marine environmental impact assessments
  • Geotechnical investigations for coastal projects
  • Innovative construction techniques for marine structures
  • Project management for large-scale harbour developments
  • Collaboration with regulatory agencies and stakeholders
